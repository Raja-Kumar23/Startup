About Pukaar

Our mission is to provide support and resources to those affected by domestic violence

Pukaar was created with a simple but powerful mission: to provide a lifeline for those experiencing domestic violence. Our app offers discreet access to resources, emergency assistance, and emotional support.

With features like Quick Exit and Voice Trigger, we prioritize your safety above all else. Our AI-powered legal advisor and mental health support ensure you have guidance when you need it most.

We believe that everyone deserves to live free from fear and violence. Pukaar is our contribution to making that belief a reality.
